About me

My name is Raymond Labban, and I am a student in Professor Layton's J465 Graphic Design II course at Indiana University in the Media School at Franklin Hall.

I definitely enjoy graphic design. But my true passions are web design and game design.

If I had to choose a specialty that I would go into, it would be website building. The reason why is that I find HTML and CSS soothing. Plus I also enjoy working in Illustrator and Photoshop, so I can create art in those softwares and incorporate them into the websites I will be building.
